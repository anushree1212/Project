Student Schemes

1. Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao (BBBP) - India's national campaign to address the declining child sex ratio and promote the education of girls.

2. Samriddhi Yojana - A small deposit scheme for the girl child's education and marriage expenses in India.

3. Child Education Fund (GCEF) - A scholarship scheme in India for the education of girls from economically weaker sections.

4. Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education (NSIGSE) - A scholarship scheme in India for promoting the enrollment of girls in secondary education.

5. Scholarship Scheme for Girl Students - In India, this scheme offers scholarships to encourage technical education among girls.

6. National Programme for Education of Girls at Elementary Level (NPEGEL) - In India, this program aims to bridge gender gaps in elementary education.

7. SABLA (Rajiv Gandhi Scheme for Empowerment of Adolescent Girls) - India's initiative targeting out-of-school adolescent girls for skill development, nutrition, and empowerment.

8. Shakti Yojana (KSY) - An initiative in India focusing on adolescent girls' health, nutrition, and empowerment.

9. Income Support Program (BISP) - In Pakistan, this program provides financial assistance to women, including support for their children's education.

10. Education Loan Scheme (TELS) - In Fiji, this scheme provides loans for tertiary education, including support for female students.
